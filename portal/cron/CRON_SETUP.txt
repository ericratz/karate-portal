----------------------------------------------------
1. WEEKLY BACKUP  (every Sunday at 7:00 AM)
----------------------------------------------------
Schedule:  min=0  hour=7  day=any  month=any  day-of-week=Sun
Command:   /usr/php74/usr/bin/php /home/sites/35b/0/049118ce4f/public_html/karate/portal/cron/backup.php

What it does:
  - Exports the full database to a .sql file
  - Saves to /home/sites/35b/0/049118ce4f/backups/karate/ (outside public_html)
  - Keeps the 8 most recent weekly backups, deletes older ones
  - Uses pure PHP/PDO — no mysqldump binary required
  - StackCP will email you output confirming tables/rows/size backed up

----------------------------------------------------
2. ATTENDANCE ALERT  (every Sunday at 7:00 AM)
----------------------------------------------------
Schedule:  min=0  hour=7  day=any  month=any  day-of-week=Sun
Command:   /usr/php74/usr/bin/php /home/sites/35b/0/049118ce4f/public_html/karate/portal/cron/attendance_alert.php

IMPORTANT: Must be Sunday, NOT Saturday.
  strtotime('last saturday') on a Saturday returns the PREVIOUS Saturday.
  On Sunday it correctly returns yesterday (the class day).

What it does:
  - Checks whether attendance was recorded for last Saturday
  - Sends you an email alert if it was NOT recorded
  - StackCP will email you confirming OK or that an alert was sent

----------------------------------------------------
3. WAIVER ALERT  (every Friday at 8:00 AM)
----------------------------------------------------
Schedule:  min=0  hour=8  day=any  month=any  day-of-week=Fri
Command:   /usr/php74/usr/bin/php /home/sites/35b/0/049118ce4f/public_html/karate/portal/cron/waiver_alert.php

What it does:
  - Checks for active students who attended in the last 7 days without a signed waiver
  - Sends an alert email listing those students if any are found
  - Exits silently (with OK message) if all attendees have signed waivers
  - StackCP will email you confirming OK or listing the students flagged

----------------------------------------------------
4. RENT REMINDER  (every Saturday at 7:00 AM)
----------------------------------------------------
Schedule:  min=0  hour=7  day=any  month=any  day-of-week=Sat
Command:   /usr/php74/usr/bin/php /home/sites/35b/0/049118ce4f/public_html/karate/portal/cron/rent_reminder.php

What it does:
  - Runs every Saturday but only ACTS on the 1st Saturday of the month
  - If rent is already recorded in expenses for this month, does nothing
  - Otherwise sends a reminder email to record rent payment
  - StackCP will email you confirming skipped / already paid / alert sent

----------------------------------------------------
WHY YOU WEREN'T GETTING EMAILS
----------------------------------------------------
Two root causes:

1. backup.php called /usr/bin/mysqldump which likely doesn't exist on
   this host. It's now rewritten to use pure PHP — no binary needed.

2. All scripts previously produced NO stdout output on success, so
   StackCP had nothing to email you. They now echo a status line
   on every run, so you'll always get a confirmation email.
